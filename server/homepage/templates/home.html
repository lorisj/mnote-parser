About me

Hi!
I am a 4th year undergraduate student studying computer science and math at the University of Michigan. I am mostly interested in quantum information theory, but also in other fields including model theory, machine learning, and algebra.

Experience

Quantum source coding

I am currently working on reasearch in quantum information theory (since May 2022) with Prof. S. Sandeep Pradhan. So far, we have developed a way to perform quantum source coding in polynomial time, and are attempting to apply this result to the problem of distributed quantum source coding. We intend to publish the results later this year.

Circuit design

In the past, I worked with Prof. Kim Kearfott on circuit design for a do-it-yourself smart geiger counter. I mostly worked on power electronics, but also worked in signal processing and software engineering.
